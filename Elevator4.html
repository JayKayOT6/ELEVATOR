CHAPTER 4
No a single speck of blood on your hands, and a sign of strain on the breathing bastard’s face a the flames engulf his body. Revenge was numbing. May this night be void form existence the same way that bastard’s ashes will be. The pastors in the country side are pretty nice, a couple of tears and here we are watching him die on this still night.
"Where there’s an end, there’s always a beginning." Devon spoke quietly.
"I agree, but the beginning I want will take some time," you smiled sadly. Gone was the civil woman who crushed on this man, there’s no way he’d accept her now. Who would employ her? There was bound to be a search on the pair soon.
"And so will mine" He sighed. "But I’m ready when you are". He looked at you with a smile as the sun’s light began to flood the sky. Feet guiding you towards his car, you rethought over his words.
"What beginning are you looking for Devon?" you asked as he smoothly manouvered over the dusty countryside roads.
"A beginning with you" the lad smiled a lopsided kittenish smile, brown eyes glimmering with sincerity masking embarrassment. A blush flooded your cheeks, gathering courage, you slid your fingers to lace into his hand resting on the gear shift.
"I wouldn’t want a beginning any better."
